thingSoC includes several capabilities that the other small form factors do not support :

  1. Production Ready, embeddable hardware platforms that are easy to use
  2. Standardized auto-discovery firmware system that identifies what boards are installed
  3. "RF Zones" specification for supporting on-board antenna and RF interfaces
  4. Built in support for battery, solar, and very low power applications
  5. High Speed Interface support, including SuperSpeed USB 3.0, PCI-E, and 10/100 Ethernet
  6. Standardized Mounting System, designed for high vibration platforms (robots, ebikes, drones)
  7. Standardized testing, instrumentation, monitoring, and data recording interfaces
  8. Interchangeable platform for break-out-boards, sensors, radios, and other devices
