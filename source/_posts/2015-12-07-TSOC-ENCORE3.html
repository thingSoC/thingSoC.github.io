It provides a complete thingSoC socket interface, including a full serial uart channel, I2C master channel, and SPI master channel with four (4) chip selects. The TSOC-ENCORE3 board is based on the Cypress Semiconductor CY7C64215, a USB Programmable System on Chip.

The Cypress Semiconductor CY7C64215 (or enCoRe III processor) is based on the flexible PSoC1 architecture and is a full-featured, full-speed (12-Mbps) USB part. Configurable analog, digital, and interconnect circuitry enable a high level of integration in a host of consumer, and communication applications.

This architecture enables the user to create customized peripheral configurations that match the requirements of each individual application. Additionally, a fast CPU, flash program memory, SRAM data memory, and configurable I/O are included

{{page.product_slug}} Features:

  • Complete Eight Pin Serial Connection (RXD, TXD, CTS, RTS, DTR, DSR, DCD, RI)
  • Solid State, Fast power limiting and protection
  • Under/Over Voltage, Over Current, and ESD protection
  • Programmable Analog and Digital Building Blocks
  • Auto-Discovery of attached peripherals and radios
  • Configuration of attached peripherals
  • Monitoring and Instrumentation of attached peripherals
  • Standard metadata store for configuration and parameter information
  • An Open-Source Hardware (OSHW) Reference Design
