thingSoC firmware is based on a virtual platform definition that supports a number of different System-on-Chip platforms and peripherals, such as cell modems, packet radios, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, and more.

{{page.product_slug}} Features:

  • Auto-Discovery of attached peripherals and radios
  • Auto-Configuration of attached peripherals
  • Auto-monitoring of attached peripherals
  • Standard metadata store for configuration information
  • An open-source hardware standard (OSHW)

We are currently have the beta release planned for May 2015.

If you are interested in participating in the thingSoC community development, please join our mailing list (it is low frequency - about once per month).
