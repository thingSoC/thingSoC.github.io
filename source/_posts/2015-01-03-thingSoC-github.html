PatternAgents has created a new Github Site for the community development of the thingSoC platform.

thingSoC is a modular development platform for the Internet of Things, that addresses many of the limitations of current product offerings; such as automatic device discovery, automatic device configuration, and self testing capabilities.

thingSoC can support existing hardware small form factors, like Xbee, Arduino, rfDuino, Raspberry Pi, and others; but adds new features and capabilites to make building your next IoT device much easier, with a more reliable final product.
