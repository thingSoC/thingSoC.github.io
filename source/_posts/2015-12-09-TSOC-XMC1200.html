It provides a complete eight (8) pin full UART connection as well as the popular six (6) pin "FTDI" connector that is compatible with Arduino and many other development boards. The TSOC-XMC1200 board is based on the Infineon XMC1200, an ARM Cortex-M0 microcontroller.

{{page.product_slug}} Features:

  • 48MHz ARM Cortex-MO with 200K FLASH, 16K SRAM, and 8K ROM Bootloader
  • UART Channel, I2C Master Channel, SPI Master Channel, five (5) ADC/Analog Inputs
  • RGB Color LED for User/Status
  • Complete Eight (8) Pin Serial Configuration Available (RXD, TXD, CTS, RTS, DTR, DSR, DCD, RI)
  • Ultra-Fast, Solid-State power protection circuit
  • Under Voltage, Over Voltage, Over Current, and ESD protection
  • Five (5V) to Three (3.3V) Level Translator with four channels (TXD, RXD, RTS, DTR)
  • Standard Six (6) Pin Serial UART Connector (5V FTDI)
  • thingSOC JTAG Connector (8 pin)
  • An Open-Source Hardware (OSHW) Reference Design

{{page.product_slug}} Applications:

  • RGB (WS2811) Color LED String Light Controller
      - Can control four (4) Strings of 5V WS2811 Lights by itself
      - (Can control more Strings with Adaptor board)

  • Add A Radio Module for Cellular, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, or Packet Radio Support
  • Add a Relay Module for remote control applications
