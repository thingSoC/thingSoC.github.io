thingSoC : a new IoT platform paradigm

thingSoC is a modular development platform for the Internet of Things, that addresses many of the limitations of current product offerings; such as automatic device discovery, automatic device configuration, self-monitoring and self-testing capabilities.

thingSoC can support existing XBee format radios, but adds many new features and capabilites that make designing your next IoT device much easier and more reliable.

thingSoC provides a vendor neutral and device agnostic hardware platform, using a virtual hardware platform definition. This means that the IoT applications you develop can be easily migrated without having to re-write your software.

What can you do with thingSoC?

What could you build with an IoT platform that is both simple to use, and ultra reliable?

  • Data Logging and Remote Monitoring
  • Asset Monitoring Solutions
  • Smart Home Applications
  • Measurement and Verification Applications

thingSoC is a new IoT platform paradigm that is different from the "rest of the pack", in that it offers seemless hardware monitoring, and verification of correct operation. No other solution out there includes the capability to remotely monitor the operation of your IoT device on a cycle by cycle basis. thingSoC will change the way you think about performance monitoring and product development and lifecycle costs.

thingSoC is completely open-source hardware and software, so you are not locked in to any proprietary solutions. Sign-up for our mailing list to here more about the thingSoC development and it's upcoming release.
